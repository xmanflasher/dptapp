## Captured Feature Lists (Summarized)

### Garmin Connect
- **Activities**: Run, Bike, Swim, Hike, Strength, Yoga, etc.
- **Health**: Sleep Score, HRV Status, Stress Tracking, Body Battery.
- **Performance**: VO2 Max, Training Status, Load Ratio, Recovery Time.
- **Planning**: Garmin Coach, Course Creator, Calendar Sync.

### Strava
- **Engagement**: Segments, Kudos, Comments, Photos/Videos.
- **Competition**: Leaderboards, Local Legends, Challenges.
- **Safety**: Beacon, Real-time location sharing.
- **Discovery**: Global Heatmap, Route Builder, Point of Interest (POI) search.
